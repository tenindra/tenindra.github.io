hiring

RIKEN offers several programs, some in collaboration with JSPS, for both overseas and Japan-based postdocs and PhD students to undertake short- or long-term research stays in Japan. If you’re interested in joining us, feel free to reach out with your CV to explore potential support for an application.

Program Applicant Type Duration Deadline
RIKEN SPDR Program Postdocs (all) 3 years ~Mid-April
JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship (Standard) Postdocs (overseas) 1-2 years Jul / Mar
R-CCS International HPC Internship PhD Students (overseas) 90 days Dec / Feb
JSPS Postdoctoral Fellowship (Short-Term) Phd Students/Postdocs (overseas) 1 to 12 months Aug / Dec / May
R-CCS HPC Internship PhD Students (Japan-based) 0.5 to 3 months May / July
RIKEN Junior Research Associate PhD Students (Japan-based) Max 3-4 years Sep to Oct
RIKEN JSPS Special Research Fellowship Postdocs (Japanese/PR) 3 years Mar
